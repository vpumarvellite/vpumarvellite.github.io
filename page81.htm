Martha Cash & Bernadette Kite

Christ the Maori

Martha with group arriving in Mosgiel

Martha was an early member of Rangimarie and went everywhere for Concerts and Christ the Maori. She was also very active in fund-raising. She and Sadie Hussey used to gather great bales of second-hand clothes to make money for the projects at Te Unga Waka. One day she got worried that Sadie hadn't phoned for a few days, so she called Bobby Newson and Pa Ryan. They climbed in the window and found Sadie lying there quite dead, with her little dog who was very pleased to see us. The house was stacked with second-hand clothing for the next money-raising effort.

There were sad moments in her life, but she transformed them by her faith. Now she lies in Waihapa, with nephew Teddy and sister Mary, both of whom she looked after for many years.

God bless you dear "Auntie" – we know you are still being an Auntie for us in heaven.
