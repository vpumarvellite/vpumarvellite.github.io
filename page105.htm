Blessings

Te Ariki leads in the Ope. Pumi Taituha and Kingi Ihaka on either side.
Blessing of Taonga to be worn in Spain. These were a gift of aroha carved by the women of Arohata Prison.
Huia present the taonga to Queen Te Ata-i-rangi-kaahu.
Piripi and Kito help Sir Kingi.
Queen Te Ata, Sir Howard Morrison, Pa Henare, Lang Davis, Max Cryer, John Turei.
